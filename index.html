About

Hello! I'm Najiya Naj, PhD Scholar at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, Delhi (IIIT-D). Under the mentorship of Dr. Arani Bhattacharya and Dr. Debopam Bhattacherjee, my research focuses on Remote operation of vehicles over different networks.

My research explores systems and networks, focusing on wireless communication and IoT systems.
