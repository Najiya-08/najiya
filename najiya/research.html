Research

My research is centered around the remote operation of vehicles over diverse network environments. I focus on understanding latency, jitter, and bandwidth limitations, and their impacts on real-time control systems.

Current Projects

  • Network-Aware Teleoperation: Developing algorithms to adapt vehicle control commands based on real-time network conditions.
  • QoS Optimization: Evaluating transport layer protocols for high-reliability teleoperation in wireless and 5G networks.
  • Edge Computing for Teleoperation: Leveraging edge servers to reduce control loop delays and improve safety.

Collaborations

Working closely with researchers in the Systems and Networks Lab at IIIT-Delhi, and collaborating with industry partners to test solutions on real-world platforms.
